About Me

I currently work in the Visual Effects industry as a Technical Director. In this position, I am responsible for developing tools / scripts that enable artists to be more productive. I am also involved in developing technology required to bring clients' artistic vision into the big screen.

Before becoming a developer / problem solver, I worked as an artist in Visual Effects, helping to make movies look awesome for a bit more than 10 years.

The journey from working as an artist to developing software for other artists helped me develop a passion to keep learning any new skill that I am curious about.

I am currently enrolled in a Front End Development bootcamp from Edx along with the Meta Back-End Developer Professional Certificate on the Coursera platform.
